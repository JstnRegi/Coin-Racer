Coin Race

Collect 4 coins to bring forth the princess, and beat your rival to her heart! Because of your money!

To start the game, click on character select!



Player 1 Controls:

W = UP
S = DOWN
A = LEFT
D = RIGHT
Player 2 Controls:

I = UP
K = DOWN
J = LEFT
L = RIGHT





Be the first to collect 4 coins too attract the princess! First one to make it to the princess wins!

Once you're comfortable with the movement controls hit Play!!
