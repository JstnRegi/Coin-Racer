Coin Race

Collect 4 coins to bring forth the princess, and beat your rival to her heart! Because of your money!

To start the game, click on the character you prefer and lock in your character.

Once you understand the controls and feel ready to start, hit Play!!



Player 1 Controls:

W = UP
S = DOWN
A = LEFT
D = RIGHT
Player 2 Controls:

I = UP
K = DOWN
J = LEFT
L = RIGHT
